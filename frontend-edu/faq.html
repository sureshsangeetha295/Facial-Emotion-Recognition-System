Everything you need to know about EngageAI Student Engagement Monitor

Is my students' camera feed stored?▼

No. Everything is processed locally. Frames are sent only for analysis and immediately discarded. Nothing is stored or shared.

Can I use it during Zoom / Google Meet / Teams classes?▼

Yes. Just run EngageAI in a separate browser tab or window while teaching. It works independently of any video conferencing tool.

How accurate is the emotion detection?▼

The model is trained on RAF-DB dataset. Accuracy is good under normal lighting and when the student's face is clearly visible. Confidence scores are shown for every prediction.

Does it require high-end hardware?▼

No. It runs efficiently on normal laptops. The heavy model runs on your local backend (CPU mode by default).

Can multiple students be monitored at once?▼

Currently it monitors one face at a time (the clearest face in frame). Multi-student support is planned for future updates.

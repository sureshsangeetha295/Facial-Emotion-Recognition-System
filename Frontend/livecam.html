About Live Analysis
Continuous Monitoring — your attention and engagement are tracked frame-by-frame throughout the entire online class session, giving you an unbroken picture of how focused you were.
Live Engagement Score — a real-time speedometer moves dynamically as the AI detects your emotional state, so you always know if your attention is drifting before it becomes a problem.
Session Timeline — every emotional shift is automatically logged with a timestamp. After class, review the full timeline to see exactly when you were most and least focused.
Live Reactions — send instant signals without interrupting the class. Use 👍 when content is great, 👎 when it's unclear, ✋ to request a repeat, 😕 if confused, or 👏 to appreciate a good explanation.
Smart Signal Alerts — if your internet connection drops or weakens, a warning banner appears instantly suggesting you move closer to your router for better connectivity.
Motivational Nudges — when you seem confused or distracted, the insight panel shows encouraging tips to get you back on track, not just a score.

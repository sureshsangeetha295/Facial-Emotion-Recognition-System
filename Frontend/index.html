Help & FAQ

Frequently asked
questions.

Is my camera feed stored anywhere?
No. Frames are sent to the local backend for analysis and immediately discarded. Nothing is stored, logged, or shared.
Which browsers are supported?
Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Any modern browser with WebRTC support. No plugins required.
How accurate is the detection?
The model is trained on the RAF-DB dataset. Accuracy varies by lighting, angle, and expression intensity.
Do I need an account?
No account needed to detect. Sign up gives you session history, saved results, and feedback features.

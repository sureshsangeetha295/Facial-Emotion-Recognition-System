All questions

Is my camera feed stored or shared?

No. Your camera feed is processed entirely within your browser session. Frames are sent to the backend only for analysis and immediately discarded after the result is returned. Nothing is stored, logged, or shared.

Which browsers are supported?

EmotionAI works on Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Any modern browser with WebRTC camera support will work. No plugins, extensions, or downloads required.

Was this helpful?
How accurate is the emotion detection?

The model is trained on the RAF-DB dataset. Accuracy varies by lighting conditions, face angle, and expression intensity. The confidence score displayed reflects the model's certainty for each prediction.

Was this helpful?
Do I need an account to use EmotionAI?

No account is needed to try the detection. Creating an account gives you access to session history, saved results, and feedback features.

Was this helpful?
Why is the detection not working?

Make sure the backend server is running with python main.py. Check that camera permission is granted in your browser. Ensure your face is well-lit and fully visible. If the issue persists, refresh the page.

Was this helpful?
What data does EmotionAI collect?

EmotionAI does not collect biometric data. No images or video frames are ever stored. The app runs locally on your machine — data never leaves your device.

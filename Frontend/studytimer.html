How Adaptive Timer Works
Focused (Happy / Neutral) — timer runs normally. Extended +2 min bonus if focused for 5+ mins straight.
Bored (Sadness / Disgust) — detected for 20s → suggests a short break. Break starts automatically after 3 alerts.
Frustrated (Anger / Fear) — timer pauses for 30s. A calming suggestion is shown with breathing guidance.
Distracted (3 consecutive low-eng frames) — timer pauses immediately and an alert overlay appears on camera.
